Google Drive Sync

Sign in with Google to automatically sync your portfolio across all your devices and browsers. Your data is saved to a private file in your Google Drive that only you can access.

What gets synced: your watchlist, holdings, fundamentals, analysis mode & custom thresholds.
What stays local: your Alpha Vantage API key (for security).
File location: a file named portfolio-watch-data.json in your Google Drive root.
Save to Drive — pushes your current data up to Google Drive.
Load from Drive — pulls the latest saved data down (overwrites local).
Auto-save — saves to Drive automatically whenever you make changes.
